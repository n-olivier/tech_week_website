Tech Week

About TechWeek

This is a week-long event spearheaded by the African Leadership University Computer Science Department that aims to empower youth to innovate and launch careers in tech and celebrate their advancements and efforts. We intend to do this by providing a platform for students, organizations and the government to meet in a series of workshops, talks, exhibitions and demonstrations and hackathons that are geared towards enhancing our shared future. All our activities are well-thought-out and contribute to the ultimate goal of skills development and exposure for our young techies.
